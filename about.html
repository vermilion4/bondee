About Us

We are for those seeking a connection - those looking for a bond.

We are here to help you make meaningful connections with people who share similar interests with you. You can search for users based on interests, age range, gender, career and location. Whether for relationships, friendships or collaboration.

Our Story

Bondee was started to encourage meaningful connections. We know how good it feels to meet people who share similar interests; from hobbies to career. so bondee is here to help you find those people around you. Download the app, complete your profile, filter/search by interests, career etc, add the connection and start a conversation !

Our Values

Accuracy

We believe in giving you the best options when you search the app. Our algorithms ensure you are matched with people you have the most in common with.

Quality

Quality connections are assured by ensuring every user is verified on the app.

Visibility

We believe everyone deserves to be seen and given a fair chance to make a connection. Every active profile is made visible to other members.

Trust And Safety

We want bondee to be a fun and safe app to make meaningful connections. The safety of our users are a priority. We moderate pictures posted and take any reports seriously.
